Update on the
PUSD Tree Campaign

Thank you for your attention and care regarding the effort to save the PUSD trees.

We are pausing our email campaign while we pursue legal action to protect the trees.

We had hoped the PUSD School Board would make a clear, science-based decision that complies with the Pasadena City Tree Protection Ordinance. Because that did not occur, our efforts are now focused on seeking immediate legal protection for the trees.

Please stay tuned for updates, and thank you again for your support.
